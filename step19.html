🛡️Title IX Campus Prevention Training

All first-time, first-year, transfer, and graduate students are required to complete the online Campus Prevention Training to avoid a registration hold.

📌 Training Details

  • The training takes approximately 2 hours.
  • You can complete it in multiple sessions at your own pace.
  • It's strongly recommended to start early and complete the sections in increments.

🔓 Accessing the Training

One week after your course registration, you can access your Title IX training and view any related holds through the SHSU training portal:

➡️ SHSU Campus Prevention Training Portal
