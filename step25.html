🚗Application for Texas Driver's License

If you plan to drive while in Texas, you will need to apply for a Texas driver's license. It’s an important step in establishing residency and legally driving in the state.

🚦 Steps to Get Your Driver’s License

  • 🖥️ Create an account and complete the online driving course at easytexasdriversed.com. The course costs $24.95 and you’ll receive a certificate upon completion.
  • 📄 After completing the course, go to impacttexasdrivers.dps.texas.gov and complete the required video course to earn your second certificate.
  • 📅 Once you have both certificates, take them to the nearest Texas Driver License Office and schedule your driving exam.
  • 🏠 If you pass the exam, your Texas driver’s license card will be mailed to your home address within approximately 10 days.

💡 Note:

Some on-campus jobs may require you to have a valid Texas driver's license. Be sure to check the job requirements before applying for positions that may require driving.
