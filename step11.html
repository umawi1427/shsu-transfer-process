Before attending your visa interview, make sure you have the following documents prepared:

  • Valid Passport: Must be valid for at least six months beyond your intended stay in the U.S.
  • Form I-20: The official document issued by your school (SHSU) confirming your admission and SEVIS ID.
  • SEVIS Fee Receipt: Proof of payment for the I-901 SEVIS fee, which confirms your registration in the SEVIS system.
  • Visa Appointment Confirmation: Printout of the appointment confirmation and interview instructions from the visa scheduling website.
  • DS-160 Confirmation Page: The confirmation with the barcode that you received after completing the DS-160 application form.
  • Academic Transcript: Official transcript from your previous institution showing your academic history.
  • CV/Resume: A brief summary of your academic and personal background.
  • Bank Statement: A recent bank statement showing sufficient funds (e.g., $39,110) to cover your educational expenses.
  • Sponsor's Bank Statement: A recent financial statement from your sponsor, showing their ability to support your education (if applicable).
  • Financial Sponsor's Work Proof: Proof of employment or income from the person who will financially support your education (if applicable).
  • MRV Fee Receipt: Proof of payment for the Machine Readable Visa (MRV) fee, required for the interview.
  • Biometric Photo: A U.S. visa-compliant photo. Review the specifications provided in the official guide below.
  • Inter-University Agreement Protocol: Download protocol.pdf
  • Course Curriculum Plan: Download course.png
  • Acceptance Letter: Download from the SHSU Self-Service Center by logging in with your credentials.
