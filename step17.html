All international undergraduate and graduate students at SHSU are eligible to work on campus during the academic year.

Job Portal: https://shsu.peopleadmin.com/

There is another website, aramark.com, which also offers on-campus jobs. The site opens later, so you can type Sam Houston in the search tab to find and apply for available positions.

  • You can log in using your LinkedIn account.
  • First, go to your profile and upload any supporting documents (e.g., resume, transcripts, recommendation letters).
  • Then navigate to the "Search Jobs" section and find positions that you qualify for.
  • Submit your application for a job that matches your background and interests.
