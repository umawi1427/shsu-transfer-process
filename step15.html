📄 Honors College Application Instructions

Once your documents are ready:

  • Log in to the SHSU Self-Service Center.
  • Upload all required documents.
  • Click "Continue" after uploads.
  • Click "Submit" to finalize your application.
  • You can track your application status in the Self-Service Center.

🌟 Benefits of Joining Honors College

  • 🎓 Opportunities for financial support
  • 🤝 Community involvement and civic engagement
  • 👥 Social opportunities with like-minded peers
  • 📘 Specialized courses designed for honors students
  • 👨‍🏫 Smaller class sizes with more faculty interaction
  • 📅 Early registration for classes each semester
  • 🏛️ Access to a private Honors Lounge and Lab
  • 🎓 Honors distinction on your diploma upon graduation
