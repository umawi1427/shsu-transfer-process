After receiving your U.S. F-1 student visa, you are now free to book your flight to Texas!

Make sure to choose your arrival date based on your I-20 document. You can enter the U.S. up to 30 days before the program start date listed on your I-20.

Houston George Bush Intercontinental Airport (IAH), is the closest major airport to Sam Houston State University (SHSU).

Tip: Booking your flight early often helps you find better prices and more flexible options.
