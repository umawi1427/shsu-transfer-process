🎓 Apply for SHSU Internal Scholarships

Now that you've applied to the Honors College, it's time to apply for internal scholarships at SHSU. Follow these steps:

💻 How to Apply for Scholarships

Visit the official SHSU scholarship portal:

➡️ SHSU Internal Scholarships Portal

Sign in using your SHSU credentials (SAM account).

📝 Steps to Apply

  • Click on "Our Opportunities" to view available scholarships.
  • Browse through the scholarship options and find the ones that match your eligibility and situation.
  • Click on the scholarship that fits your needs and follow the instructions to apply.
  • Make sure to submit all required documents and information as per the scholarship application guidelines.

📅 Deadlines

Ensure that you submit your scholarship applications before the deadline to increase your chances of being awarded financial support.
