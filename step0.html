If you have not yet taken an approved English language proficiency test, you can take the Duolingo English Test as part of your application process.

  • Visit the official website: englishtest.duolingo.com
  • The test can be taken online from home using a computer with a camera, microphone, and internet access.
  • The duration is approximately 1 hour and includes a short interview and writing tasks.
  • Results are usually available within 48 hours.
  • A score of at least 105 is required.

Tips:

  • Practice in advance using free resources provided on the Duolingo website.
  • Ensure you're in a quiet, well-lit room with no interruptions during the test.
