To graduate with an Honors diploma, you must:

  • Maintain a cumulative SHSU GPA of 3.25.
  • Attend Honors Advising once per semester.
  • Maintain progress toward completing honors requirements.
  • Complete 18 hours of Honors contracts/courses:
    • Honors Courses
    • Honors Contracts FAQ
  • Complete 6 hours of Honors Seminars.
  • Engage in at least 10 hours of community service per semester (optional but highly encouraged).
  • Advance Registration: Completing the 10 hours of service each semester qualifies you for early registration. Honors students who meet this requirement are the first to register when registration opens. If you donâ€™t complete the hours, your registration follows standard class-level scheduling.
  • New students: Advance Registration is granted during your first semester. For example, a Fall 2021 incoming student can register early for Spring 2022, even without prior service. However, you must complete and submit a Community Service Form during your first semester to maintain eligibility for future semesters.
  • Attend at least 3 Honors events per academic year.

To graduate with Highest Honors, you must meet all Honors graduation requirements plus:

  • Complete a senior thesis in an approved discipline under the direction of a faculty member of your choice.
  • Learn more about the thesis process here: Honors Thesis FAQ
